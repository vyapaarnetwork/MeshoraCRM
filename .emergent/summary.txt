**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **User Roles (4):** Selling Partner, Sales Associate, Customer, Super Admin.
- **Registration:** Only Customers can self-register. Other roles are created by the Super Admin.
- **User Management:** Super Admins can create, edit, delete, and assign users to companies.
- **Master Data:** Admin-managed data for Lead Status, Categories, etc.
- **Lead Management:** Leads support customer details, assigned partners, status tracking, follow-up dates, comments, and document uploads (proposals, etc.).
- **Draft Leads:** Leads created without a Selling Partner are in Draft status.
- **Lead Referral:** Selling Partners and Sales Associates can refer leads, which are created in Draft status.
- **Partner as Customer:** Selling Partners can create Internal Request leads for services from other partners.
- **Follow-ups:** Must specify if pending with Customer or Selling Partner.
- **Commission Logic:** Complex commission calculation rules.
- **Dashboards & Reports:** Role-specific dashboards and detailed, filterable reports (Vyapaar Performance, Partner Business, etc.). All data grids should be sortable and filterable.
- **Notifications:** An in-app notification system (bell icon) for lead updates and assignments.
- **SMS Notifications:** Send a WhatsApp/SMS message (via Twilio) to the Selling Partner and Super Admin upon lead assignment.
- **Document Management:** Users can upload corporate profiles for companies and attach documents (with tags) to leads. These documents should be viewable and downloadable.
